Bio



Litty deBungus is a 6+ piece Funk ensemble featuring a powerful horn section, soulful vocals, and a tight rhythm section that’ll blow your mind. Whether it's a song about love, loss, or gettin' down - Litty Debungus creates a rollercoaster of emotions and brings the audiences along for the ride.


From backyard parties, breweries, and bars - Litty deBungus has developed a dynamic live performance that invites every listener to move their body and join the party. With influences ranging from Tower of Power, Rush, Lettuce and the California Honeydrops - Litty Debungus blends classic funk style with psychedelic rock ‘n’ roll


Litty deBungus has released 4 singles on all platforms and will follow it up with their debut album by the end of 2022






Noah Stid - Guitar and Vocals

Stefan Turkowski - Guitar and Vocals

Brett Warren - Bass

Evan Angelico - Drums and Vocals
