Biography


Litty deBungus is a 6+ piece Funk ensemble from Oakland, CA - first formed in 2018. Litty D inspires people of all kinds to move their bodies and their minds. With bass-driven rhythms, powerful horn section, searing guitar solos, and soulful vocals - the party never stops.

With influences ranging from Tower of Power to Primus and Lettuce to the California Honeydrops - Litty Debungus blends classic funk style with psychedelic rock ‘n’ roll. Litty deBungus has released 4 singles on all platforms and will follow it up with their debut album by the end of 2022
